Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Social Media Assistant ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Apple ID (encrypted identifier provided by Apple Sign-In)
  • Display name from Apple Sign-In
  • Email address (only if you choose to share it through Apple Sign-In)

Usage Data

  • Number of captions generated daily (to enforce 10-caption limit)
  • Date and time of app usage
  • Selected caption preferences (mood, length, subject)

Technical Data

  • Device type and iOS version
  • App performance and crash data
  • Network connection status

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI-powered caption generation services
  • Enforce daily usage limits (10 captions per day per user)
  • Maintain and improve our services
  • Ensure account security and prevent abuse
  • Provide customer support when needed
Important: We do not permanently store the captions you generate. They are processed by our AI service and returned to you, but not saved in our databases.

Data Storage and Security

Storage

  • Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We follow industry-standard security practices
  • Data centers are located in secure, monitored facilities

Data Retention

  • Usage data: Daily usage counts reset automatically each day
  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Generated captions: Not permanently stored on our servers

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database services
  • OpenAI: AI caption generation (your content is not stored by OpenAI)
  • Apple Sign-In: Secure authentication services

Each service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in our system
  • Correct any inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data (though minimal data is stored)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, and safety
  • With your explicit consent
  • In connection with a business transfer or acquisition

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Users

If you are accessing our service from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States and other countries.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications (if provided)
  • Updates to this page with new "Last Updated" date

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it.
